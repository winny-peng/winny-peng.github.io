For the Fall 2023 semester, I currently work as a teaching assistant at the University of Toronto, where I grade student assignments and/or lead classroom activities (tutorials). My goal is to pursue a career as a User Experiece (UX) designer or researcher. My initial introduction to the world of UX stemmed from my interest in video games and game design. However, technology is heavily dominant in our everyday lives, and I've learned that UX is more ubiquitous that I previously thought. From web design, strategic planning, mentorship and running my own business, I like to apply a lot of UX concepts to improve the experience for my deliverables, clients, students, or even customers. I've always thought I wanted to join the entertainment industry to create products that brought me joy when I was growing up. But I want to think outside of entertainment, and focus more on fixing everyday pain points. I believe that by simplifying and improving user experiences in everyday applications or projects, people can live happier, more effiecient lives.
