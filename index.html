Hobbies & Interests

I like to do a lot of things. Some are useful, many are useless. Here are some of my more useful hobbies and interests.

Journaling

I enjoy journaling and hoarding stationery products. To fund my expensive hobby, I started a stationery shop where I sell hand-drawn penguin stationery. Check out my shop's instagram and online store!

Video Games

I play video games in my free time to destress. My current go-to game is Valorant or Ace Attorney (when I have some spare brain power). Many of my passion/side projects include game design; check them out here.

Puzzles

Throughout the day, I enjoy solving sudoku and crossword puzzles. Cracking The Cryptic is my favourite source of variant sudokus. Some day, I want to learn how to set puzzles myself and create "impossible" jigsaws like Yuu Asaka.

Music

I like listening to music, especially k-pop and musical theatre. I found the best way to combat my boredom in the shower is to re-enact the entire Phantom of the Opera and pretend I sound half as good as Sierra Borgess.

Travel

Despite being a homebody, I love travelling and I get strangely excited whenever I visit a new hotel, motel, inn, airbnb, tent, or even someone's guest room. Nothing beats coming back to a nice cosy bed after a day of exploring :)

Food & Cooking

I love to eat and I think cooking is the perfect median between science and art. One day, I will open up a penguin themed cafe or food truck, and develop recipes that are fun to make and fun and eat.
